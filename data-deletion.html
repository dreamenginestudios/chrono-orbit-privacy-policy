Data Deletion Request — Chrono Orbit

Developer: Dream Engine Studios

App: Chrono Orbit (package name: com.dreamengine.chronoorbit)

How to request deletion of your data

Send an email to dream.engine.studios.de@gmail.com with the subject line "Chrono Orbit — Data Deletion Request".

Since the app does not require a username, email, or password to play, please include in your message:

  • The approximate date you started playing and/or your in-game nickname (if you set one in Profile settings), so we can locate your account record.
  • Optionally, a screenshot of your in-game Profile screen, which helps us confirm the record.

We will process your request and confirm deletion by email within 30 days.

What data will be deleted

  • Your anonymous account identifier (Firebase Authentication UID)
  • Your saved game progress stored on our server (gold, crystals, keys, owned skins, upgrade levels, mission progress, leaderboard entries)
  • Any nickname or avatar selection associated with your account

What may be retained

  • Aggregated, non-identifying analytics events already processed by Google Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob prior to the deletion request (these are not linked back to you individually once deleted).
  • Records of in-app purchases that Google Play itself retains for financial/legal/tax compliance purposes, independent of our app (governed by Google's own policies, not controlled by Dream Engine Studios).

Local data

Uninstalling the app from your device immediately deletes all locally stored save data. This page covers deletion of the copy of your data stored on our backend (Firebase), which uninstalling alone does not remove.

See also our Privacy Policy.
